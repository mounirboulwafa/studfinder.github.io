What is Stud Finder?

Stud Finder – Wall Scan turns your iPhone into a simple wall-scanning tool. It helps locate metal fasteners commonly found in wooden studs, as well as some metal pipes or conduits, using your phone’s built-in sensors.

Designed for DIY projects and home mounting, the app provides real-time visual, audio, and haptic feedback while you scan.

Key Features

Live Wall Scanning Real-time magnetic strength meter while moving across the wall.
Edge & Center Guidance Helps identify the edges and center of detected metal objects.
Haptic & Audio Feedback Feel and hear changes as you approach stronger signals.
Calibration & Sensitivity Adjust sensitivity and recalibrate for better results.
Scan History Save scans locally for future reference.
Privacy-Friendly No accounts, no tracking, no data leaves your device.

Important Limitations

Stud Finder is designed to assist — not replace — professional tools.

  • Detects ferromagnetic metals only (steel nails, screws).
  • Cannot reliably detect wooden studs without metal fasteners.
  • Does not safely or reliably detect live electrical wiring.
  • Accuracy depends on wall thickness, phone model, movement speed, and interference.

Tips for Best Results

  • Move your phone slowly and steadily.
  • Calibrate before each scan.
  • Avoid nearby metal objects.
  • Scan the same area multiple times.
